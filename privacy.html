Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Universal AI LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates IQ: The Mental Game ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: First name (via Apple Sign-In)
  • Profile information: Basketball position, level of play (e.g., high school, college, pro), and personal goals
  • Game logs: Game performance entries you create (stats, reflections, notes)
  • Chat messages: Conversations with the AI coach

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: App opens, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Subscription status: Active plan type and billing period (managed by Apple and RevenueCat)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Last name or full legal name
  • Email address (Apple Sign-In may hide your email; we do not store it)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or camera/microphone data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Coaching: Your chat messages are sent to OpenAI to generate personalized coaching responses.
  • Personalization: Your position, level, and goals are used to tailor the training experience.
  • Progress Tracking: Game logs and activity data power your Mental Score and skill progression.
  • Push Notifications: We send daily reminders to support your training routine (you can disable these in Settings).
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage data helps us improve the App.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App. Each processes only the data necessary for its function:

OpenAI
(GPT-4o-mini)		 AI coaching responses Chat messages only. Messages are sent via API for response generation. Your data is not used to train OpenAI models.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than operating the App.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Storage: Your data is stored on your device and on Supabase's secure cloud servers.
  • Retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Security: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen within the App. Account deletion will:

  • Permanently remove your profile, game logs, chat history, and all associated data from our servers.
  • This action is irreversible.
  • Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Please cancel your subscription through Apple before deleting your account.

6. No Advertising or Tracking

The App does not contain any advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or analytics services that profile you for ad targeting. We do not collect or use Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA).

7. Your Rights

All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

GDPR Rights (European Economic Area)

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to restrict processing, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Our lawful basis for processing is your consent (provided when you create an account) and legitimate interest (operating and improving the App).

CCPA Rights (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.
  • We do not sell personal information. Therefore, we do not offer an opt-out of sale.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@universalai.app.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@universalai.app.

9. Push Notifications

The App may send push notifications for daily training reminders and motivational prompts. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings or within the App.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
