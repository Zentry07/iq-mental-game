Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID).
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Find IQ: The Mental Game and tap it.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription.

Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

How do I request a refund?

Since all payments are processed through Apple, refund requests must go through Apple directly:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID.
  3. Find the IQ charge and select "Request a refund."

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly in the App:

  1. Open IQ: The Mental Game.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

This permanently removes your profile, game logs, chat history, and all data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Important: Deleting your account does not cancel your subscription. Please cancel your subscription through Apple first (see above).

How does the AI coach work?

The AI coach is powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model. When you send a message, it is processed by the AI to generate a personalized coaching response based on your position, level, goals, and conversation history.

The AI coach can help with:

  • Pre-game mental preparation and visualization
  • Post-game analysis and reflection
  • Building confidence and managing pressure
  • Developing focus, discipline, and competitive mindset
  • Working through slumps, setbacks, and self-doubt

Note: The AI coach provides general mental performance guidance. It is not a licensed therapist or psychologist, and its responses should not be treated as professional mental health advice.

What is the Mental Score?

Your Mental Score is a personalized rating that tracks your mental game development over time. It is calculated based on your engagement with training exercises, game log reflections, AI coaching sessions, and daily habits. The score is designed to motivate consistent practice, not to diagnose or evaluate your mental health.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not run ads or use tracking tools. Your chat messages are sent to OpenAI only to generate coaching responses and are not used to train AI models. You can delete all your data at any time by deleting your account. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use the app offline?

Some features like reviewing past game logs may work offline, but AI coaching, syncing, and real-time features require an internet connection.
